My Projects

Farm Productivity Data Analysis Project

In this project, I analyzed agricultural survey data to identify the factors influencing farm productivity among smallholder farmers. I used data analysis techniques to interpret patterns and generate practical recommendations for improving productivity.

Household Income and Food Security Study

This project focused on examining the relationship between rural household income and food security. I explored how economic conditions affect access to food and overall household welfare in rural communities.

Agricultural Market Access Research

This study looked at the challenges farmers face in accessing markets, including transport, pricing, and market information. The aim was to understand how better access can improve incomes and rural livelihoods.
