My Biography

I am Lucky Movin, a student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Agricultural Economics and Rural Development at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT). I have a strong interest in agricultural research, rural transformation, and the use of data analysis in solving challenges facing farmers and rural communities.


I enjoy working with data to understand issues such as farm productivity, household income, food security, and market access. Through my studies, I have developed skills in data analysis using SPSS and Stata, and I am interested in applying these skills to real-world agricultural and rural development problems.
